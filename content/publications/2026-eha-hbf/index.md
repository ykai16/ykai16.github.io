BMS-986470 is a novel CELMoD™ (Cereblon E3 Ligase Modulating Drug) agent currently being investigated for the treatment of sickle cell disease. By modulating Cereblon to degrade key transcriptional repressors of the γ-globin genes, BMS-986470 potently induces fetal hemoglobin (HbF) expression—a clinically validated therapeutic strategy for ameliorating disease severity.

In this work, we combined **genome-wide epigenomic and transcriptomic profiling** to dissect the molecular basis of HbF induction by BMS-986470. Our analyses characterized the downstream transcriptional and chromatin remodeling events that underlie its potent HbF-inducing activity, and compared its mechanism of action to other CELMoD agents in the class.

This work was presented at the **European Hematology Association (EHA) Annual Congress 2026** in Stockholm, Sweden (June 2026).
